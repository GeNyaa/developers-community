What will you achieve at the Tel Aviv Summit?

The LiveEngage Open Platform offers our customers the unique opportunity to enhance the consumer care experience. Join our experts for an intensive 24 hours of cooperative development!

Your influence will help shape the products that your business will later use. Create fully realized products and take exclusive advantage of them right away.

Frequently Asked Questions
  • What is the Co/Dev Summit? - The Summit offers you the opportunity to collaborate with our development and Technical Solutions teams to create products on top of LiveEngage during 24 hours of intensive development.
  • Who Should Attend? - operations/digital channel managers, developers/IT people, product designers.
  • Who is LivePerson Bringing? - Product Management, R&D and Product Designers, Technical Solutions Engineers.
  • When & Where? - The Co/Development Summit will be taking place November 15-16, 2017 in Tel Aviv, Israel.
  • What if I want to attend but can't make it to Tel Aviv? - Feel free to message us below and we'll work it out!

From our hands to yours, each team will receive access to top-of-the-line knowledge and experience in order to develop solutions like:

  • Agent Surveys - develop a solution to better poll your agents for their quality of life needs and pain points.
  • Post-Engagement Surveys - enable your agents to send customized post-engagement surveys on the fly.
  • Dashboards - design and launch a useful dashboard, encompassing all your contact center's KPIs.
  • Routing Bot - create an efficient bot that routes your visitors to the right skill and the right agent at the right time.
